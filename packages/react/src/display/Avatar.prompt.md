**Avatar** — represents a person or entity; shows an image, or initials derived from `name`.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Asha Rao" src="/photo.jpg" size={48} status="online" />
<Avatar name="Public Grievance Cell" shape="rounded" />
```

- `src`, `name` (initials fallback), `size`, `shape` (`circle`/`rounded`), `status`.
